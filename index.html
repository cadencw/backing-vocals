About Backing Vocals

Founded in 2024, Backing Vocals is a student-led outreach initiative through 5 Melody Music School dedicated to connecting generations through music. We organize live performances, coordinate volunteers, and collaborate with senior care facilities to create personalized, meaningful musical experiences.
